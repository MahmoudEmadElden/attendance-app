# How to Get Your App Link (Deploy to Glitch)

**The code is now on your GitHub!**
Repo: [https://github.com/MahmoudEmadElden/attendance-app](https://github.com/MahmoudEmadElden/attendance-app)

## Final Step: Get the Mobile Link

1.  **Go to [Glitch.com](https://glitch.com/)** and sign up/login.
2.  Click **"New Project"** -> **"Import from GitHub"**.
3.  Paste your repo URL:
    `https://github.com/MahmoudEmadElden/attendance-app`
4.  **Wait a moment** for it to install.
5.  Click **"Share"** -> **"Live App"**.
6.  **Copy that link** and send it to your phone.
